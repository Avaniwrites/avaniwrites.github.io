Introduction

Hey!

As a former educator with a strong foundation in interdisciplinary learning in Zoology and M.A. in English, I've developed a passion for exploring the intersections of science, language, and education. With a year of formal teaching experience and three years of working as a freelance tutor, I've honed my skills in creating engaging learning experiences and staying updated on innovative methodologies. In addition to this, I have worked on a Biodiversity Survey of different panchayats conducted by the Department of Rural Development, HP, in Community Development Block Nahan, District Sirmour.

Research Experience

My research endeavors, including authoring two research papers, "Women's Sexuality and Identity Crisis: between Past and Present: Reading 'My Story' by Kamala Das" and "Dalit among Dalits: A Study of Joothan and The Prisons We Broke," have further deepened my interest in academic inquiry. Now, as I transition into a research-focused path, I'm eager to pursue a PhD, leveraging my teaching expertise and research background to contribute meaningfully to the field.

Academic Interests

I am an enthusiastic researcher and write articles and papers to offer insights on social problems. As a student of Humanities, I write for the betterment of society in my free time. I also have a passion for theatre, often attending plays and even preparing students for their own theatrical performances.

My academic interests lie in the Romantic era, Victorian literature, postcolonial studies, and feminist studies, with a particular focus on Indian literature. I'm an avid reader, with a deep appreciation for the works of authors like Harper Lee, Khaled Hosseini, Albert Camus, and Sylvia Plath. Their thought-provoking writings have not only broadened my perspectives but also inspired me to explore the complexities of human experience and the nuances of social justice through my own writing.

Creative Pursuits

Beyond academia, I find inspiration in creative pursuits like reading books, painting, writing, and poetry, and I enjoy discussing detailed analyses of books and movies with my peers.

Personal Background

Growing up in the Himalayas, I've developed a deep love for nature, which I nurture through gardening, travel, and spending time outdoors. I am a keen observer and thinker, often trying to connect the dots and understand things on a deeper level. I have a habit of questioning things and seeking their answers on my own through reading, interacting, and discussions.

Apart from this, I try to volunteer for the underprivileged people of my village, offering free lessons and counseling.

Note

I seek a funded PhD opportunity, and I'm excited to merge my academic rigor with my love for research and creative expression.
